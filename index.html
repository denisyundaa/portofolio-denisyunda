About Me

Hello, I'm Denis Yunda Putri Kurnia.

Beyond the world of code, the camera lens is my close companion. I am active as a freelance Videographer, Camera Operator, and Video Editor. Whether I'm developing an elegant user interface or producing cinematic video, my goal is always the same: to create memorable, high-quality work.

Case Study: PT. Mitra Buana Suradinata Truck Rental Website

The Challenge

As part of a 3-month research internship, the objective was to develop an efficient web-based system for PT. Mitra Buana Suradinata to manage their truck rental operations, specifically focusing on an administrative dashboard that streamlines order processing and vehicle management.

My Role

I was responsible for the backend development, designing and implementing the admin management dashboard, and handling the entire database management. This included creating APIs for data interaction, ensuring data integrity, and developing the core logic for order tracking, status updates, and vehicle inventory.

Team Collaboration

  • Taufiq: Spearheaded the frontend development, ensuring an intuitive and engaging user interface for both customers and administrators.
  • Dini: Managed the research report aspect of our internship and served as the key liaison for communication between our intern team and the company.

Technologies Used

  • PHP for Backend Logic
  • MySQL for Database Management
  • Apache Server
  • HTML5 for structure
  • CSS3 & Bootstrap 5 for styling (Frontend)
  • JavaScript (Frontend)

The Outcome

The project resulted in a robust and user-friendly administrative dashboard capable of efficiently managing truck rental orders, tracking their statuses (pending, paid, cancelled), maintaining vehicle inventory, and generating comprehensive transaction reports. My contribution significantly improved the operational workflow for the company's internal management.
